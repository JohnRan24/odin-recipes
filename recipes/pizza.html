Pizza

This is a recipe on how to make a pizza with these ingredients and simple steps

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast. to ensure the yeast is still active by sprinkling it over warm warter ina bowl
  2. Make the dough. you can use a food processor to combine dry ingredients, then slowly add liguid ingredients while the motor is running. The dough should form a ball and rotate int eh bowl without sticking.
  3. Shape the dough. Handle the dough gently to preserve air pockets created during fermenetation.
  4. Add Sauce. Spread a thin layer of sauce over the dough. Pizza sauce is a key ingredient that binds all the other flavors together.
  5. Add Toppings. Add pepperoni on the pizza and add other toppings if desired.
  6. Bake. Preheat the oven to at least 500 F, and bake the pizza for less 15 minutes. You can use a pizza stone or baking steel sprinkled with cornmeal. Start by placing the pizza in the middle of the oven to see if it cooks faster on the top or the bottom.
